Introduction

After dealing with some things, I now finally have time to develop a new Minecraft RTS game.

Below are some of my thoughts on the architecture and mechanics of this game.

Resources

Types

There are 3 resources in total: manpower, wood and gold.

How to gather?

All our territories except forests and mines can provide manpower.

Wood and gold can be gathered in occupied forests and mines.

Territory

Composition

A territory is made up of 9X9 blocks.

The entire map is expected to consist of 12X12(144) territories.

Occupy

Occupying territory can increase income, prevent enemy expansion, and provide usable building space.

The territory must be connected to the command center to use it.

If it is not connected to the command center, the territory will not produce resources, and the buildings will not be able to be used and constructed.

(Maybe I can add a flag on it after occupied?)

Build

Buildings can only be built on occupied territories, and different buildings have different functions.

If the building is destroyed by the enemy, the territory will also be lost.

Lost territory

If the core of the territory is destroyed, the territory will be restored to its original state.

Control

Chat bar control

This time the player will play the game from a bird's-eye view, so the player is in spectator mode and cannot control inventory.

Therefore, the control method will be changed to be determined by height.
